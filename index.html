Citizens Concerting Football Club

Founded in 2025 and based in Babadogo — we are a passionate group of colleagues and friends who gather every weekend to play, compete and grow together. We believe football builds teamwork, resilience and community.

Founded 2025 Babadogo Colors: Blue & Red

About Us

Our mission is to promote sportsmanship, team spirit and wellbeing through regular football activities among colleagues and locals.

We train regularly, participate in local friendlies and organize social events. Everyone is welcome — players, supporters and volunteers.
