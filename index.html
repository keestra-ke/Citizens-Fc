About the Club

Founded in 2025 and based in Babadogo, Chandaria Converting Football Club (C.C.F.C.) is a passionate team of workmates and friends united by the love of football. We play every weekend for leisure, competition, and camaraderie — promoting sportsmanship, teamwork, and growth among colleagues both on and off the pitch.

⚽

Our Mission

To promote football excellence and friendship among our community, one match at a time.

🤝

Our Values

Teamwork, respect, dedication, and sportsmanship in every game. Always give a friend a second chance.

🏆

Our Goal

To compete at the highest level while maintaining our close-knit community spirit.
